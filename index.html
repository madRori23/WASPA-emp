Upload Instructions:

  1. Click the "Login to Google Drive" button above
  2. Sign in with your WASPA Google account
  3. Upload your manual test video recordings to the appropriate folder
  4. Ensure video files are named according to WASPA naming conventions:
    • Format: testtype_network_servicename_date.mp4
    • Test Type Codes:
      • ct - Complete test
      • pt - Partial test
    • Example: ct_mtn_playvod_30.10.2024.mp4
    • Example: pt_vodacom_hooperstv_15.11.2024.mp4
  5. Supported video formats: MP4, MOV, AVI, WMV
  6. Maximum file size: 2GB per video
